"One man's garbage is another man's treasure" is a perfect way to introduce ourselves. Read more about us using this website and purchase our subscription using the arrow!

Why Would I Subscribe To This Service

  • Removes Waste

    Waste is a major issue in the world we live in. It's covered in garbage and things that shouldn't be there. Rather than contributing to that, why not work on preventing such a thing?

  • Benefits the Future

    Composting puts all of that waste right back into the Earth, helping future plants and life grow better than you could ever imagine.

  • Helps Businesses

    Our compost gets transported right to farms, greenhouses, and more to further help them succeed.

What Compost Is Used For

  • Better Soil

    Laying compost down makes the soil exponentially more rich allowing plants and crops to grow much better.

  • Less Work for Businesses

    Farms and greenhouses alike will have a much easer job growing plants and food as well as producing better plants and food.

  • Overall Cleanliness

    With composting the world will have less waste covering it which will make for a much better and cleaner environment for us to live in.

Fun Facts About composting

  1. Certain species of birds use composting to produce heat while incubating their eggs.

  2. A lot more things are compostable than you think! Nail clippings, dryer lint, and even clothes are some of the strange ones just to name a few.

  3. Composting helps break down those dirty contaminants in the soil and water.
